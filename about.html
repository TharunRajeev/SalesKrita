About

Every salesperson has a genius within him or her. The entire team in SALESKRITA strongly believes in the hidden potential of every member in the sales organization – frontline salesperson or frontline manager or middle manager or top management team. Uncertain business environment, competitive pressures and undue demands from the customers hid the potential in a salesperson. We help them unearth the true potential which is intrinsic.
